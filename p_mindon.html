Social & Emotional Learning

MindOn is a comprehensive Social and Emotional Learning (SEL) program which focuses on developing the ideal environment to nurture students to bring out the best in them.

Mind On has three goals that are its pillars

1. Develop self-awareness and self- management skills to achieve school and life success
2. Building decision-making skills and responsible behaviors in personal, school and community contexts
3. Use social-awareness and interpersonal skills to establish and maintain positive relationships

Tippie

Visual Identity, Visual Language
For Triangular Studios Pvt. Ltd. Mumbai, INDIA

Your Digital Wings:

This product is a tribute to all those who've tried hard to get their parents or their grand parents to use digital services only to fail miserably. And then there were those who were thoroughly irritated while addressing each and every one of their parent's queries. We Heard you..!! Tippie's got your back!


Tippie is the smartest way to master the smartphone. It lets everyone, who finds it a bit difficult to stay in sync with rapidly changing times, stay updated and have fun!

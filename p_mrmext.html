My Mumbai My Local

localsofmumbai.com


Sharing information, thoughts and experiences about Mumbai Sub-urban Railway.

The extended project for MRM (Mumbai Rail Map) is to collectively communicate information about Mumbai rail to people through various media, this project includes various possible implementation strategies for MRM Projects - Mumbai Rail Map (Print) & MrmApp (Mobile Application). #LoveMumbai is a campaign to create a platform for sharing and knowing about travel related information, thoughts and experiences around the Mumbai local journeys.

Mumbai Rail Map

मुंबई रेल नक्शा . ممبئی ریل کا نقشہ . मुंबई रेल नकाशा . મુંબઈ રેલ નક્શો

An extensive journey that started over a year back with two design students dreaming to give the cosmopolitan city of Mumbai a Rail Map has led us this far. Since the first Mumbai Rail Map launch in December 2013, the project has grown due to the perseverance of a motivated team, and the encouragement and feedback from the people of Mumbai. The latest version 6 of the MRM are available for download in Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Urdu & English languages.

MrmApp

After 6 Versions in 5 different languages of Mumbai Rail Map, MRM Mobile App released on feb 14th 2015. Developed for iOS and Android, the MrmApp is a step to further assist the commuters with a smoother travelling experience by providing all the required information available at a tap.

Jaikishan Patel

Communication Designer


Loves to observe, experience & design. Focused in Communication Design, believing if there is an honest communication, life can be understood so easily.

Loves to observe how various communication happens around. Respects the value addition and attention to details. Highly concerned about the brand language and its behavior.Born and brought up at Raigarh in the region of Chhattisgarh INDIA. Graduated in 2011 from the department of communication design at D J Academy of Design, Coimbatore. Did post graduation in visual communication at Industrial Design Centre, IIT-Bombay, Mumbai.
